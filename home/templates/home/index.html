Our Story

Get Wurst is run by Paul and Lindsay Melbourne, who spent many a trip to Berlin eating bratwurst on the streets in the early hours. Get Wurst was then born in 2015 out of a frustration of not being able to get a proper bratwurst in the UK. Well, now you can, dishing up the good stuff at events and festivals across the North, and at our new home in Sheffield city centre at the top of Fargate, where you can enjoy some serious German street food 7 days a week from lunch till late.
